Question: EXPLAIN ME THIS SOP

**User:** EXPLAIN ME THIS SOP

**Assistant:** This SOP (Standard Operating Procedure) outlines the steps for annotating A+ Content to train a Quality Score 2.0 model. The model will be used for downstream treatments, including tiered CX treatment and customized recommendations.

The main objectives of this SOP are:

1. To collect 1K annotation data by March 31, 2025, for Q1'25.
2. To measure accuracy via human label.

To achieve these objectives, the annotation task will be handled by 3-5 different individuals independently, without seeing the results from other teammates.

The SOP outlines 7 parameters to conduct checks on general A+ content quality:

1. Error-free text
2. Defect-free image
3. Redundancy-free
4. Rich product feature
5. Effective module usage
6. Style consistency
7. Image authenticity

The text review includes checks for:

1. Grammar and spelling
2. Text scannability
3. Redundancy

The text review also includes specific examples of common defects, such as grammatical errors, spelling errors, awkward language, and punctuation issues.
