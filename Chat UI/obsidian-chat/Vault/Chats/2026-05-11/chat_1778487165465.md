**Assistant:** This SOP outlines the steps for annotating A+ content to prepare for training an AI model called A+ Quality Score 2.0. Here's a breakdown of the main points:

**Objectives:**

1. Annotate rubrics for A+ Content.
2. Use the annotated data to train the A+ Quality Score 2.0 model.
3. Apply the trained model for downstream treatments, such as:
	* Tiered CX treatment in detail pages.
	* Customized recommendations to eliminate defects.

**Version History:**

The SOP has two versions:
1. **Version 1**: Created on 2/24/2025, which marks the basic SOP creation.
2. **Version 1.1**: Updated on 5/19/2025, which clarifies text readability.

**Review Instruction:**

To ensure accuracy, the annotation task should be handled by 3-5 different individuals independently, without seeing the results from other teammates.

**Checks:**

The SOP outlines 7 parameters to conduct checks on A+ content quality:
1. **Error-free text**: Ensure the text is free from errors.
2. **Defect-free image**: Ensure the images are free from defects.
3. **Redundancy-free**: Ensure the content does not contain redundant information.
4. **Rich product feature**: Ensure the product features are well-presented.
5. **Effective module usage**: Ensure the modules are used effectively.
6. **Style consistency**: Ensure the style is consistent throughout the content.
7. **Image authenticity**: Ensure the images are authentic and not manipulated.

**Overall Feedback:**

After completing all checks, provide an overall feedback with reasons if any defects are found.
