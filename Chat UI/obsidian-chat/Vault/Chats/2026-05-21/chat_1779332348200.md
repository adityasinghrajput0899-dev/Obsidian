In this context, normalization refers to the process of converting attribute values into a standardized format, making them consistent and easily understandable. This includes:

* Correct capitalization (e.g., 'black' instead of 'Blk')
* Accurate spelling (e.g., 'water resistant' instead of 'WR')
* Use of accepted abbreviations (e.g., 'stainless steel' instead of 'SS')
* Avoiding unclear abbreviations (e.g., 'foldable' instead of 'FOL')

Normalization helps to ensure that product information is presented consistently and accurately, making it easier for customers to understand and compare products.
