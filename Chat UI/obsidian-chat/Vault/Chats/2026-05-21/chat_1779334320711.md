**Redundancy** occurs when words or phrases are triplicated within modular titles. A title exhibits redundancy when any term appears more than two (>2) repetitions. In other words, it occurs when a word or phrase appears three or more times (≥ 3). However, for the purpose of this SOP, we will consider repetition of a word more than five times (≥ 5) as a potential case of redundancy.

**What constitutes Redundancy?** 
 - PT and item name repetition 
 - Singular and Plurals 
 Words with accents (non-EN languages)
**What does not qualify to be Redundant?** 
 - Connecting words (articles, prepositions, conjunctions)
 - Synonyms of PT or item name or any attribute
 - Contextual repetitions
