**New Section: Removing Wrong Modules**

To remove wrong modules, follow these steps:

1. Identify the wrong modules used in the content.
2. Replace the wrong modules with the correct ones.
3. Ensure that the correct modules are used strategically to enhance the message.

**Example:**

* Wrong module: FAQ as image
* Correct module: FAQ as text

**Result:**

* The content is more informative and easier to understand.
* The customer can make more informed purchase decisions.
