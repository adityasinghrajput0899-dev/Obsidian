**Hallucination** means Starfish model is adding words, terms, or details to the **modular title** that were **not present in the original title, bullet points or product description**. These invented additions are usually inferred by the model and can **misrepresent the product** and **mislead customers**. 
Update on **Hallucination Scenarios**
Specific scenarios are observed in the Starfish model where modular titles should be evaluated carefully for hallucination:
1.** Paraphrasing / Reformation **
This occurs when the model enhances the language, grammar, or phrasing of the modular title without changing the original meaning. This includes replacing words with synonymous or functionally equivalent expressions to improve clarity or readability. These are purely lexical improvements where the semantic meaning remains unchanged. Examples:
