5. How to make final decision?
Perfect A+ projects are hard to come by. The final decision of a project being 0 (low-quality) vs 1 (not-low-quality) is a
combination of the attributes you see above, weighted by how much they impact shopper experience.
A project with minor defects yet still brings value to shoppers should be considered 1, whereas the project that provides no
value add or has major defects should be considered 0.
